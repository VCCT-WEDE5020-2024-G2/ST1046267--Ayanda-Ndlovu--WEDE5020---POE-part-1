Welcome to Kitara, a compassionate animal welfare organization dedicated to protecting, nurturing, and rehoming animals in need. Kitara was founded in [2003] by [Jack Foreman], driven by a shared passion for animal welfare. Our journey began with Jack,Ayanda,France, Ryan, Lily,Francina,Stijn,Daan and Loek when they realised how animals were being treated and there was no one who would stand up for them .Today, we operate a state-of-the-art shelter, provide comprehensive veterinary care, and engage with the community through education and outreach programs. Kitara's mission is to provide safe haven, medical care, and loving support to abandoned, injured, and vulnerable animals. We strive to create lifelong connections between humans and animals through adoption, education, and community engagement.
